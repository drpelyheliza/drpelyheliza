Where data tells stories

As an interdisciplinary researcher and biostatistical consultant, I build bridges between biology, materials science, and data analysis. With a PhD in Mechanical Engineering, MSc degrees in Biology and Biomedical Engineering, and a Business Data Analyst qualification, I transform complex datasets into clear, practical insights. My strength lies in understanding the language of medicine, the logic of engineering, and the rigor of statistics – and in applying them together.
